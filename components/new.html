WHAT WE BELIEVE

>

We believe that there is only one God, who revealed Himself to humanity in the person of Jesus Christ.

Deuteronomy 6:4; 1 Timothy 3:16; John 14:9

We believe that believers are called to live a holy, pure life before God and all humanity.

2 Corinthians 6:14-7:1; Hebrews 12:14

We believe that salvation is only found in Jesus Christ and is appropriated to humanity by God's grace through the New Testament new-birth experience.

Acts 4:12; Ephesians 2:8; John 3:3-7

We believe that the church will be raptured by Christ prior to the great tribulation and will exist with Him forever in eternity.

1 Thessalonians 4:15-18; 1 Corinthians 15:51-52; 1 Thessalonians 1:10

We believe that the new-birth experience is comprised of repentance, water baptism in the name of Jesus Christ, and the infilling of the Holy Spirit. This is the New Testament pattern for all believers to follow as a result of their faith in Christ.

Acts 2:38; Luke 13:1-5; Acts 10:44-48; Acts 19:5-6

We believe that God's power is still active in the church and that healings, signs, and miracles are available to God's people through faith in Jesus Christ.

Hebrews 13:8; 1 Corinthians 2:4; 1 Peter 2:24

We believe that when a person receives the baptism of the Holy Spirit, that person will speak with other tongues under the inspiration of the Holy Spirit.

Acts 2:4; Acts 19:6; Isaiah 28:11

Mission: Changing the Entire Brentwood Community one life at a time through a genuine Apostolic lifestyle!

Vision: The River Community Church was created to help people from all walks of life to go deep in their relationship with God with a true Pentecostal experience. Ezekiel 47

Lead Pastors

Kirt & Pamela Denney have served in full-time ministry for over 25 years. In addition to ministry at the local level, they have been clinicians in workshops, seminars and spoke at conferences both at home and overseas.

Kirt & Pam have a passion for ministering the gospel as well as a deep love for marriage and family. Over the course of their ministry, they have mentored hundreds of couples and helped many of them prepare for marriage, or save their marriage. Probably at the top of the list is a deep love for the presence and power of God. They love to help people get connected to their potential in Christ as they take the next steps in their spiritual journey.

Pamela is a gifted speaker, singer and musician. In addition, Pam has a heart for empowering and mentoring women to be all they can be in the Kingdom of God. She frequently teaches classes and Bible studies directed at specifically helping women.
With their two sons Jonathan and Keagan, they currently live in Franklin, TN and serve as the pastors of The River Community Church in Brentwood.

Pam is a Certified John Maxwell Teacher/Speaker and Coach.

Kirt is a graduate of Christian Life College and holds an MBA.
