Ways To Give

  • Text Giving- From your phone text MCONL to 77977
  • Marketable Securities– Transfer of ownership of stocks, bonds, or mutual funds.
  • Real and Personal Property– The best way to capture the value of appreciated property and get a tax- deduction at the same time, donate jewelry, fine arts, cars, boats, or even real estate.
  • Planned Gifts– a great estate planning tool, name Metro Church as a beneficiary of your term or permanent life insurance policy, retirement accounts, Will, or various types of Trusts. There are potential ways to great tax benefits now rather than later.
  • Matching Contributions– See if your employer will match your contributions.
